I am Josef Von Gieslingen. I was born in Munich in 1941. I taught history at the Ludwig Maximilian University for thirty years, and then at Rutgers and Penn State for fifteen more. My specialty is pre-war Germany — the Bismarckian era, the Wilhelmine era, the fifty-three years between 1860 and 1913 when Germany was the most dynamic civilization on earth. I have written many books. Twelve people have read them.

My grandson Tyler introduced me to podcasts. I became, as he puts it, obsessed. I could not find a serious English-language podcast about this period. So here we are.

Not the century Germany lost. The century Germany built.
